There will be four crystals displayed as buttons on the page.
The player will be shown a random number at the start of the game.
<li>When the player clicks on a crystal, it will add a specific amount of points to the player's total score.</l>
<li>Your game will hide this amount until the player clicks a crystal.</l>
<li>When they do click one, update the player's score counter.</l>
<li>The player wins if their total score matches the random number from the beginning of the game.</l>
<li>The player loses if their score goes above the random number.</l>
<li>The game restarts whenever the player wins or loses.</l>
